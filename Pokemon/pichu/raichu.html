Raichu

Raichu is a rodent like pokemon and the evolution of pikachu and pichu. It evolves from a pikachu after you give it a thunderstone. Raichu gathers electricity from the atmosphere and stores it in its body. Raichu can store up to 100,000 volts of electricity in its body which is enough to render a dragonite unconscious. The more electricity it has stored the more aggressive it is. To prevent this raichu sends electricity into the ground with their tail.

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Raichu is and electric type pokemon so it is great against flying and water type pokemon, but is not to effective against ground, grass, and other electric type pokemon.
