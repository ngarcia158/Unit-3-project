Mewtwo

Mewtwo is a result of years of gene splicing and DNA engineering experiments from the pokemon mew. It is suspected that ditto are the failed results of the experiments because they are the only other pokemon besides mew that can use transform. Due to the way that it was engineered the creators failed to give mewtwo a compassionate heart. With them forgetting that mewtwo only desires battle to prove its strength.

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Mewtwo is a psychic type pokemon so he is weak to dark, bug, and ghost type pokemon. But he is effective against fighting and poison type pokemon.
