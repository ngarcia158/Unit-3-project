Charmander

Charmander is a fire-type Pokemon his body is orange colored and a pale belly. His health can be determined by the flame on his tail which will burn brighter the more healthy it is, it also displays Charmanders emotions it burns brighter the more angry and if he growls it means he is going to attack. The more his tail burns the more damage they do. Charmander is the nicest and most well behaved out of his evolution line. Doesn’t like being rushed so they can’t deal with impatient people but extremely loyal. Can be found in the wild but very rare because it is a starting Pokemon but can be found near hot areas such as active volcanoes or in craggy mountains and in caves .

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Since Charmander is a fire type pokemon he is weak to ground and water type pokemon. But he is effective against fairy, grass, steel, ice, and bug type pokemon.
