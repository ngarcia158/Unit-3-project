Pichu

Pichu is a rodent like Pokemon it can store electricity in its cheek pouches but only small amounts it can discharge the energy but will also shock itself. They are very social pokemon and like to be in groups. It evolves into Pikachu after getting a high friendship with it and will further evolve into a Raichu if you give Pikachu a thunderstone.

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Pichu is and electric type pokemon so it is great against flying and water type pokemon, but is not to effective against ground, grass, and other electric type pokemon.
