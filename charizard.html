Charizard

Charizard is a flying-fire type pokemon that is orange with wings and has horns. It has a long neck and small blue eyes also two fangs. His fire on his tail is so great it can stay on underwater and his fire burns brighter the more xp he gains.

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Since Charizard is a flying-fire type pokemon he is weak to electric, water, and rock type pokemon. But he is effectiv against grass, bug, fighting, steel, ice, and fairy type pokemon.
