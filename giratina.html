Giratina

Giratina is a dragon, ghost type pokemon that is large gray draconic Pokemon with golden rings coming from his back and has a crowned shaped ring on his head. He also has two large horns on his head this pokemon is so powerful that it has the power to control and travel through dimensions beyond time and space.

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Since Giratina is a dragon-ghost type pokemon it is weak against ice, dragon, ghost, fairy, and dark type pokemon but is efective against dragon, ghost, and psychic pokemon.
