Charmeleon

Evolves from charmander at level 16 and evolves into charizard at level 36. The pokedex refers to the pokemon as “When it swings its burning tail, it elevates the temperature to unbearably high levels.” He has dark red scales, It has narrow, blue eyes and a long snout with a slightly hooked tip. On the back of his head he has a single horn.

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Since Charmeleon is a fire type pokemon he is weak to ground and water type pokemon. But he is effective against fairy, grass, steel, ice, and bug type pokemon.
