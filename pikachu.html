Pikachu

Pikachu is a chubby rodent like Pokemon that can store electricity in itself. Unlike its pre evolution form pichu it doesn't hurt itself while discharging energy. You can tell the difference between a male and female pikachu by looking at the tail. The female pikachu will have a heart shaped tail end while the male will be flat.

Battle advantages and disadvantages

Pikachu is and electric type pokemon so it is great against flying and water type pokemon, but is not to effective against ground, grass, and other electric type pokemon.
